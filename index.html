The Creation Of The Universe

First the universe started off really small with only gas, and gradually space expanded into itself, and we started off as tiny little super hot particles. Eventually those particles expanded into different elementary particles, and then a fight broke out between matter and antimatter, but matter kept winning so that generated more protons and neutrons. After that, hydrogen started to form, which formed galaxies and stars, and much later, humans started to become humans.
